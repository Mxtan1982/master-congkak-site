1. Overview

MASTER CONGKAK is a casual educational game. We do not collect or store personally identifiable information (PII) from users directly. The app may display third-party advertisements via Google AdMob.

2. Data We Process

  • Advertising & Analytics (via Google Mobile Ads SDK / Google Play services): device and app identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), approximate location, and coarse usage data may be processed to show ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.
  • No account, login, or contact data is collected by us within the app.

3. Third-Party Partners

We use Google AdMob / Google Play services. Their processing is governed by their own policies and your device settings:

  • Google AdMob & Google Play services – Advertising & cookies policy
  • Ad personalization controls – Google Ads Settings and your device’s “Ads”/privacy settings (e.g., “Opt out of Ads Personalization”).

4. Children

If the target audience includes children under 13, we configure AdMob accordingly (e.g., TFUA/“tag for child-directed treatment”) and limit data processing for personalized ads where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We do not keep personal data on our own servers. Third-party partners may retain pseudonymous data per their policies.

6. Security

We rely on the platform and SDK providers (Google) for storage and transmission security of advertising/analytics data. We do not maintain backend servers for this app.

7. Your Choices

  • Use the device setting to reset or limit the Advertising ID.
  • Disable personalized ads via system privacy settings or the Ads Settings page above.
